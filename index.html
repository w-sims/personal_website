William Sims

I am a computer science PhD student under Dr. Meera Sitharam at the university of Florida. My current research is in computational geometry and combinatorial rigidity. I work on problems such as finding paths in configuration spaces and characterizing the propagation of defects in lattices. These problems are fundamental to molecular structural physics, chemistry, and biology.
